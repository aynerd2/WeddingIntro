## Inspiration for Future Couples

### What to Include
- Your story and how you met
- Beautiful photos (pre-wedding, engagement)
- Wedding party with fun facts
- Event details and timeline
- Registry or gift information

### Customization Ideas
- **Colors**: Match your wedding colors
- **Fonts**: Choose fonts that reflect your style
- **Photos**: Professional + candid moments
- **Features**: RSVP, livestream, guestbook
- **Languages**: Translate for international guests

### Technical Tips
- Optimize all images before uploading
- Test on multiple devices
- Set up analytics to track visitors
- Use custom domain for professionalism
- Enable HTTPS for security
